Graduated in Social Communication, with a focus on design. Works with communication and design since 2010, focusing on the design of visual identities, digital interfaces and editorial projects. Besides studying communication, he is enthusiastic about new technologies involving art, urbanism, cinema, cycling and coffee. He currently works at Axur as a Product Designer. He is also a professor at the Technical Visual Communication course at Univates.
